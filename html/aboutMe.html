about me

My name is Dima, I am 18 years old. I was born in Ukraine, and now I live and study in Slovakia at the University of TUKE.

Programming was initially my hobby and interest. I started with creating games, the hobby appeared at the age of 12.

Then I couldn't figure it out without help, and I started learning Python, knowing that at that time it was the most popular programming language.

At the age of 15, I switched to learning HTML and CSS. Within a year I was learning algorithms, learning Python and backend in depth. In the last two years until I was 18, I decided to focus on frontend as that was the direction I was most attracted to.

At the age of 16, my hobby turned into trying to make a living at it.

For a long time I didn't take orders because I have a drive to do things with quality, especially for myself.

At the moment I feel very confident in frontend and I think I can fulfill 85% of orders, although I still have little real experience.

I learned frontend from video tutorials, pet projects and orders for people I know.

I enjoy doing complex artistic websites, and I'm eager to further pump my skills by learning new techniques, effects, and animations.
